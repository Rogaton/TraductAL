## Why Champollion Couldn't Be Replaced by AI

### The Fundamental Difference

**Statistical AI approach:**
- Input: Large corpus of hieroglyphic texts
- Method: Find patterns, predict sequences
- Problem: **No Rosetta Stone at scale**, no understanding of *what the symbols mean*
- Result: Plausible-looking gibberish

**Champollion's approach:**
- Input: Deep knowledge of related languages (especially Coptic)
- Method: **Linguistic intuition** seeing Egyptian roots through Coptic vocabulary
- Insight: Coptic preserves ancient Egyptian in Greek script
- Result: **Actual decipherment** of a dead language

**The critical difference**: Champollion had a **theory of the linguistic relationship**. He understood that:
- Coptic is the latest stage of Egyptian
- Greek script was adopted for a fundamentally Egyptian language
- The phonological and lexical connections could bridge 2000+ years
- Grammar structures would show continuity

**No amount of compute could generate this insight from data alone.**
